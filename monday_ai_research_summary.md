## Two things to flag

- **Screenshots** — same as Linear and ClickUp: no monday MCP / playwright tooling exposed in this session. I left labelled `[screenshot slot]` placeholders for the 2 most natural visual spots (Sidekick floating assistant + Skills Marketplace). 17 recommended screenshots in the raw notes file — drop them in and inline-base64 them, or share separately.
- **The "All In on AI" repositioning is the headline.** May 6, 2026 reframes monday from a "work management platform" → **"AI Work Platform."** This is the most aggressive marketing-language pivot of any PM competitor in 2025–2026. Eran Zinman called it "the biggest change in the history of our company." Alongside it: a new AI Credits pricing model that becomes mandatory for new customers from May 6 (Sidekick metering May 20, Agents metering June 8) — which is also the biggest pricing-related user complaint. The Agentalent.ai marketplace (March 23, 2026) is the most differentiated strategic move — monday positions itself as "the AI agent hiring platform for the enterprise," a category no PM competitor has fully claimed.

Ready to move to the next competitor — **Asana** (AI Teammates, AI Studio, Smart Workflows, Work Graph, Asana Gov) or **Notion** (Notion AI 3.0, Autonomous Agents, AI Meeting Notes, AI Search) — whenever you say.
